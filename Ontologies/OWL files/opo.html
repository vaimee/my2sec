The Open Project Ontology is inteded as a semantic representation of the open source tool Open Project. This vocabulary describe in generic manner the whole managment for projects, in term of resources and spent time. The OPO is developed as part of a stack of ontologies in the context of the My2Sec project, an activity tracking application ables to aggregate activities according with the task assigned on Open Project.
